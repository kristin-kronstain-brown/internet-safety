# Red flags

A quiet house does not mean everything is ok. Look for these signs that something might be wrong.

- They're on the phone constantly.
- They panic or are scared if they're missing something.
- Their habits change.
- They retreat.
- Look for depression or excessive self-blame.
- Look for abuse or neglect.


## What to do

Maybe not in every single case, but many times explicit photos can be taken down.

Go through their followers and ask who each person is. Look for changes in their demeanor with each name.
