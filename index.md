# A Parent's Guide to Internet Safety

Teach your kids how to live safely in an constantly connected world.


## A Simple Family Framework

You can teach almost everything by encouraging your kids to think through these five questions:
- Who is asking?
- Why do they want this?
- How does this make me feel?
- What could happen if I share this?
- Would it be okay if this became public?

That framework scales from kindergarten through adulthood. The rest of this guide uses those questions as a foundation.
