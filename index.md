Teaching kids how to live safely in an constantly connected world.


## A Simple Family Framework

You can teach almost everything through five questions:
- Who is asking?
- Why do they want this?
- How does this make me feel?
- What could happen if I share this?
- Would I be okay if this became public?

That framework scales from kindergarten through adulthood.
