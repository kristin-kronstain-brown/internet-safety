# Chat tactics

These are common tactics for manipulation in online chats:
1. Fraud
2. Coercion
3. Force

A strategy for acquiring control within conversation:
1. Zero in on what the kids want.
2. Build trust. Trust is built through history or repoir.
3. Fill a need. Get control by "providing" financially or with hope.
4. Pull them away, isolate them from others in their lives.
5. Abuse using the tactics above.
    - AI can be used to generate images for blackmail purposes
    - Boys especially can be won over with the value of digital currency
    - Don't accept phones or stuff from others you don't know.
        - "Your parent won't let you do what you want on your phone? I'll send you another one."
6. Total control.

## Teach kids basic marketing techniques

- Figure out what people want from you.
- Watch commercials and talk about what they are appealing toward and what they want out of you.

## Provide strategies for getting out

Firm "No". It's OK to feeling like you're being rude when you're protecting yourself. Safety is more important than politeness.
